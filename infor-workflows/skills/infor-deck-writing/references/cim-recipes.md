# CIM Recipes

The Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM) is the long-form sell-side document. Detailed, fact-rich, neutral-positive in tone. Multiple sections, each typically 1–3 slides.

Most CIM slide types (Investment Highlights, Company Overview, Business Segments, Industry, Process, Financial Summary, Management Team, Valuation Commentary, Buyer-List Commentary) are cross-cutting — load [`building-blocks.md`](building-blocks.md) for those. This file covers the CIM-specific opening / framing language.

---

## CIM — Executive Summary

**Purpose:** Multi-page section opening the CIM. Establishes the Transaction context, the Company, and the investment thesis before deeper sections (Investment Highlights, Company Overview, Market, Financials, Process).

**Structure:**
1. **Transaction Overview** — engagement line, purpose of CIM, contact for INFOR, key dates
2. **Key Process Milestones** — bidder timeline (Confidentiality Agreement → EOI → IOI → Management Presentations → LOI → Confirmatory Diligence → SPA → Close)
3. **Company at a Glance** — single-page company snapshot mirroring the teaser page 1
4. **Investment Highlights** — see [`building-blocks.md`](building-blocks.md) → Investment Highlights

**Opening line patterns:**

> The Company has engaged INFOR Financial Inc. ("IFI") as its exclusive financial advisor and intermediary in connection with a Transaction with the Company (the "Process").

> Responsive Brands Inc., dba Mister Transmission International ("Mister Transmission", or the "Company"), has engaged INFOR Financial Inc. ("INFOR") as its exclusive financial advisor in connection with a potential divestiture of up to 100% of the shares of the Company (the "Transaction").

> The sole purpose of this Confidential Information Memorandum (the "CIM") is to provide an overview of [the Company] to assist interested parties in evaluating a potential Transaction.

**Process restriction language (verbatim):**

> Interested parties are precluded by the Confidentiality Agreement from having discussions or communications of any kind in respect of [the Company] or the Process with any person.

> Officers, management and employees, customers, vendors and other business partners of the Company shall not be contacted about this opportunity under any circumstances without INFOR's prior written consent.

> The Process will be managed in accordance with procedures that will be established and communicated to interested parties from time to time by IFI.

**Length budget:** Executive Summary section spans 4–8 slides in a CIM. Each individual slide carries 80–250 words of body text.
