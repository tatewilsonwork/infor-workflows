# Strategic Review Recipes

Strategic reviews lay out the Company's situation and possible paths forward (status quo, M&A, financing, recapitalisation). Less promotional than a CIM, more diagnostic.

For section content (Company Overview, Industry, Valuation Commentary), see [`building-blocks.md`](building-blocks.md). This file covers the strategic-review-specific framing.

---

## Section Structure

**Typical structure:**
1. **Situation Overview** — where the Company stands now (operations, balance sheet, market position)
2. **Strategic Alternatives Considered** — a 2×2 or table comparing options
3. **Detailed Analysis of Each Alternative** — one section per option
4. **Recommendation / Next Steps** — INFOR's recommended path with rationale

**Tone:** Less promotional than CIM. Acknowledges challenges directly. Hedged forward-looking statements throughout.

**Verbatim opening pattern:**

> "INFOR has been engaged by [Company] to undertake a strategic review of the Company's options. This presentation summarises INFOR's analysis of the strategic alternatives available to [Company] and provides recommendations for next steps."

---

## Strategic Alternatives — Common Options

Typical alternatives evaluated:
- **Status Quo / Organic Growth** — execute on the current plan; benchmarks the others
- **Sale (Full / Majority)** — sell to a strategic or financial buyer
- **Minority Investment / Capital Raise** — bring in a partner without ceding control
- **Recapitalisation** — dividend recap, refinancing, or shareholder buyout
- **Merger / Combination** — combine with a complementary platform
- **IPO** — if the Company is at the right scale and profile
- **Wind-Down / Divestiture of Underperforming Assets** — focus the remaining business

**Comparison table headers (typical):**

| Alternative | Strategic Fit | Valuation Range | Timing | Execution Risk | Cash to Shareholders |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Option] | [High / Medium / Low + 1-line] | C$[X]MM – C$[Y]MM | [N–M months] | [High / Medium / Low + 1-line] | [Form + timing] |

**Recommendation paragraph pattern:**

> "Based on the Company's [current position], [market conditions], and [shareholder objectives], we recommend [pursuing / further evaluating] [Option X]. This approach [reasons — value capture / risk / strategic optionality]. INFOR recommends the following next steps: [3–5 concrete actions, sequenced]."
