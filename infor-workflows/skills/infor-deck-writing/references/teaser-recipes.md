# Teaser Recipes

The Teaser is the 1–2 page sales document distributed to potential buyers as the first piece of marketing material. Highly compressed; aspirational but defensible.

For cross-cutting slide types referenced here (Investment Highlights, Company Overview, KPI tiles), see [`building-blocks.md`](building-blocks.md).

---

## Cover Line

**Purpose:** The two-line title under the company name on the teaser cover. The first line is the Project name, the second is a 6–14 word positioning headline.

**Structure:**
```
Project [Codename]
[Positioning headline — 6–14 words, Title Case, no period]
```

**Verbatim examples:**
- `Project Lightning` / `Leading B2B SaaS Digital Wellbeing Platform`
- `Project Meridian` / `Leading Engineering Services Platform`
- `Project Daytona` / `Investment Opportunity in Disruptive Plant Protein Production`
- `Project Thunderbird` / `Specialty Automotive Services Franchisor`
- `Project Meridian` / `AI-Driven Legal Platform with Rare Caselaw Backfile Upside Post-Litigation`

**Pattern menu for the headline:**
- `Leading [adjective] [sector] Platform`
- `[Sector] Leader with [Differentiator]`
- `Investment Opportunity in [Disruptive / Resilient / High-Growth] [Sector]`
- `Differentiated [Sector] Platform with [Specific Edge]`

**Pitfalls:** Don't include the city, the year, or the deal size in the cover line. Don't use a question. Don't include the actual company name (the project codename obfuscates it).

## Page 1 — "The Opportunity"

**Purpose:** A single page that summarises the company, the transaction, key figures, and 4–5 investment highlights. The first thing a buyer reads.

**Structure (literally in this order):**
1. **Engagement line** at top, defining the Company and the Transaction
2. **Company Overview** — 3–5 sentence paragraph
3. **Key Figures** — 5–6 KPI tiles (large number + 2-word caption)
4. **Investment Highlights** — 4–6 numbered or headline-bulleted items, each with a 4–8 word positioning header and 1–3 supporting bullets

**Engagement line — verbatim skeletons:**

> Project Lightning (the "Company") has engaged INFOR as its exclusive financial advisor in connection with exploring financing alternatives which may result in: i) a financing through the issuance of new equity securities, ii) refinancing the Company's existing debt package, or iii) a combination of the above.

> Faba Canada Ltd. ("Faba Canada" or the "Company") has engaged INFOR Financial Inc. ("INFOR") as its exclusive financial advisor in connection with a potential capital raise of C$25MM, enabling the Company to execute on its growth plan via the build out of the Company's facility, secure required human capital and support working capital.

> Project Meridian (the "Company") has engaged INFOR Financial Inc. ("INFOR") as its exclusive financial advisor in connection with a potential majority divestiture of the shares of the Company (the "Transaction"). The purpose of this Teaser is to provide an overview description of the Company to assist potential buyers in their consideration of a submission of an Expression of Interest ("EOI").

Fill in:
- Project codename or real name
- Defined short term
- Transaction type — sale / majority divestiture / minority investment / capital raise of C$XXMM / financing / recapitalisation / strategic review
- Optional "(the Transaction)" defined term
- Optional purpose-of-teaser sentence

**Company Overview paragraph — verbatim examples:**

> Located in Edmonton, AB, Faba Canada has created a portfolio of superior plant-based protein and co-products from faba bean for the food industry; these new ingredients solve taste, allergenic and functional challenges food companies have in using soy, wheat and pea alternatives. The Company is complete with a production facility, established supply relationships, key industry partners and full food certification; the process is modular, and the Company is ready to scale production to participate in the healthy eating, high protein diet market.

> The Company is a leading software-as-a-service ("SaaS") provider of whole-person wellbeing solutions for employers, health plans and other organizations. The Company's holistic product suite of digital solutions allows organizations to provide best-in-class content and expertise at scale, empowering their employees to live their healthiest lives.

> The Company is a leading Canadian MEP firm providing fully-integrated mechanical and electrical engineering services and energy performance contracting ("EPC") with 60+ years of operational excellence.

**Recipe — Company Overview paragraph:**
1. Sentence 1: location anchor (city / country) + "leading" positioning + sector + product/service
2. Sentence 2: what the platform does or what differentiator it has, with one defined acronym if useful
3. Sentence 3 (optional): the scale anchor — number of customers, employees, revenue, history
4. Sentence 4 (optional): the immediate growth lever or strategic positioning

**Investment-highlight item — verbatim examples (positioning header + supporting bullets):**

> **Competitive Moat via Differentiated Platform**
> - Unique all-in-one wellbeing platform that covers all key modalities with limited competition
> - Combination of scalable technology, vast proprietary content and unique customer service model results in strong user engagement and customer retention

> **Favourable Industry Dynamics Support Growth**
> - Government-led infrastructure investment and sustainability mandates are driving increased need for engineering design and EPC services across Canada
> - The Company is well positioned to benefit from rising regulatory standards, carbon reduction targets and public commitments

> **Predictable & Profitable Model**
> - Plays a critical role in connecting / enhancing wellbeing programs and is highly regarded by its customers
> - Solutions delivered via an asset-light business model with multi-year contracts result in strong, consistent margins, with further operating leverage yet to be realized

**KPI tile blocks — examples (figure + caption):**

| Figure | Caption |
|--------|---------|
| `~C$46MM` | `CY2024E ARR` |
| `~C$12MM` | `Run-Rate EBITDA` |
| `~900` | `Unique Customers` |
| `~90%` | `Adj. Net Dollar Retention` |
| `+13MM` | `Lives Supported` |
| `C$106.3MM` | `FY2025A Revenue` |
| `C$17.4MM` | `FY2025A Adj. EBITDA` |
| `8.0%` | `Revenue CAGR` |
| `60,000+` | `Faba Acres in Network` |

Captions are 2–3 words. The figure carries its own scale (`~`, `C$`, `+`, `%`).

**Length budget:** The whole page is ~150–250 words of body text. If you've written more, you've overshot.

## Page 2 — Financial Summary, Key Contacts, Disclaimer

**Purpose:** Round out the teaser with chart placeholders for financial summary, a key-contacts table, "Platform Core Features" or similar callouts, and the disclaimer.

**Structure:**
- Title bar same as page 1 (Project Codename + cover line)
- 4 chart blocks (Revenue, EBITDA & margin, Volume/Capacity, Gross Margin) — each labelled
- **Key Contacts** table — 4 INFOR bankers, name + title + phone + email
- **Platform Core Features** — 4–6 short feature blocks (icon + 2-line description)
- Disclaimer at the bottom (verbatim — see [`../SKILL.md`](../SKILL.md) Section 4)

**Chart titles — verbatim examples:** `Revenue (C$MM)`, `EBITDA (C$MM) & Margin (%)`, `Volume (000s MT) & Capacity Utilization`, `Gross Margin (C$/MT)`, `Monthly Recurring Revenue (C$000's)`, `ARR Segmentation (FY2025A)`, `Unlevered Free Cash Flow`.

**Platform Core Feature block — verbatim examples:**
> **Scalable SaaS Technology** — Proprietary technology and delivery systems that can be easily deployed as individual modules or as a fully integrated suite; easily configurable by non-technical users

> **Deep Proprietary Database of Content** — Thousands of proprietary resources from subject matter experts covering high-impact topics; wide breadth & depth of easily consumable and compelling resources to drive health outcomes and user engagement

## Worked Example — Full Teaser Page 1 Draft (Fictional Company)

**User request:** "Draft the page 1 text for a teaser. Company: NorthBeam Energy, a Calgary-based provider of grid-scale battery storage solutions in Canada. Transaction: minority capital raise of C$40MM to fund 3 new sites. Run-rate revenue ~C$22MM, EBITDA margin ~25%, 12 sites operating, 8 in development. Strong tailwinds from federal clean-tech incentives."

**Draft:**

**Cover line:** `Project Helios` / `Differentiated Grid-Scale Battery Storage Platform`

**Engagement line:**
> NorthBeam Energy Inc. ("NorthBeam" or the "Company") has engaged INFOR Financial Inc. ("INFOR") as its exclusive financial advisor in connection with a potential minority capital raise of C$40MM to fund the build-out of three additional grid-scale battery storage sites across Canada (the "Transaction").

**Company Overview:**
> Headquartered in Calgary, AB, NorthBeam is a leading Canadian developer and operator of grid-scale battery storage solutions, with 12 operating sites and 8 sites in active development across Canada. The Company's vertically-integrated platform combines proprietary battery management software with long-term offtake agreements with regulated utilities and independent power producers; the Company is well positioned to capitalize on federal clean-tech investment tax credits and provincial grid modernisation mandates.

**Key Figures:**

| Figure | Caption |
|--------|---------|
| `~C$22MM` | `Run-Rate Revenue` |
| `~25%` | `EBITDA Margin` |
| `12` | `Operating Sites` |
| `8` | `Sites in Development` |
| `100%` | `Contracted Capacity` |

**Investment Highlights:**

> **Differentiated Platform with Vertical Integration**
> - Proprietary battery management software combined with in-house EPC capability; delivers superior site economics versus pure-play developers
> - Vertical integration provides cost and timeline advantages, with average time-to-energisation ~30% faster than industry benchmarks

> **Long-Dated, Contracted Cash Flows**
> - 100% of operating capacity contracted under long-term offtake agreements with investment-grade counterparties; weighted-average contract life of ~12 years
> - Capacity payments structured to provide predictable, inflation-linked revenue regardless of merchant power prices

> **Favourable Industry Dynamics Support Growth**
> - Federal Clean Technology Investment Tax Credit (30% refundable) and provincial grid modernisation mandates create a multi-billion-dollar TAM for grid-scale storage in Canada through 2035
> - Aging coal retirement schedules and rising intermittent renewables penetration drive structural demand for fast-response storage

> **Actionable Near-Term Growth from Funded Pipeline**
> - 8 sites in active development representing ~C$200MM of incremental capacity, fully permitted with executed interconnection agreements at 6 sites
> - Transaction proceeds will fund three priority sites with targeted commercial operation in [Quarter YYYY]

> **Top-Tier Management Team**
> - Founder / CEO previously led grid infrastructure development at [Prior Company] with 25+ years in Canadian power markets
> - Leadership team brings combined 75+ years of utility-scale project development, financing, and operations experience
